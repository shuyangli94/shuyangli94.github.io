Cookie Crumblies Instructions


Left click on a part of the cookie to send a crack running along the cookie!


Right click on a part of the cookie to crumble a section of the cookie away, feeding it to the ants.


The ants appreciate bite-sized chunks of cookie, and they won't accept cookie chunks that are too large!


Crumbling sections with chocolate chips will grant you a score multiplier for the current cookie.


When a cookie is completely crumbled, or when less than 5% of the cookie remains, you will be given a new cookie.


You may also reset your statistics, crumbles, cookie, and score at any time by clicking the RESET button.


Have fun!
